About us

We opened in 1918 as a small pizza shop catering to factory workers, and for 100 years we've been a go-to place for a quick bite in Greenwich Village and become a local landmark. We served from mob bosses to kings throughout our long history. Our loyal customers and the local press praise our dishes making us proud servers of the community. If you're ever in the neighborhood, try our delicious traditional Italian flavors ranging from famous breadsticks to large pepperoni pie. Our current address is 1 Washington Pl., New York, NY 10000, and our telephone number is (111) 111-1111.
